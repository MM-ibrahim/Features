Features

Clock

The clock is very important for everyone, it helps us to organize our daily life . You can know the time and date by voice. And you can set an alarm on the smart mirror by sound so that you will be notified at the exact time .

Calendar

It is a system of organizing days. This is done by giving names to periods of time, typically days, weeks, months and years.

Weather

Weather is something everybody deals with, and accurate data of it like what is coming can help users to make inform decisions. And display the needed clothes to wear.

Prayer Times

It displays the prayer times on the screen and remind people at the time of praying either by notification on the smart mirror screen or by voice alert.

Google Fit

Google fit is a health-tracking platform developed by Google.It is an application that shows your steps per day .

Water Reminder

This module job is to remind you from time to time to drink water, this helps you to be a healthy and balanced body.

Spotify

Spotify module is used to play music from the spotify account of the user by some voice commands, but this require that the account is to be premium. The music may be a specific song or any song of a particular artist or a playlist.

Screencast

This module cast the current playing video on you tube on smartphone into the smart mirror.

News

The newsfeed module is one of the default modules of the Smart Mirror. In order to provide the user with a few news events, a simple news feed will be displayed to provide three headlines from world news.
